My favorite animals

  • Cats
  • Basilisk
  • Panther

Cats are great animals because they don't require a lot of attention. But they are very tame, so therefore they are really easy pets to have.

Basilisks on the other hand are really cool, they may be mythical, but it still creates fear in the minds of the people.

Panthers are just bigger and less tame versions of cats. If you grow with a cub however, they would not be hostile to you.
